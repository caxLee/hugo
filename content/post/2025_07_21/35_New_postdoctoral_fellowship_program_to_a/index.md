**摘要**: MIT HEALS launches Biswas Postdoctoral Fellowship Program to advance health and life sciences research with a focus on innovation in health care. The program aims to support exceptional postdocs in leveraging AI, developing diagnostics, and interdisciplinary collaborations. Funding of $12 million will award five four-year fellowships annually starting in 2026.
