MIT's Mission Innovation X (MIx) bridges the gap between startups and government defense agencies by developing educational programs, supporting research, and facilitating connections among various stakeholders. Led by Gene Keselman, Sertac Karaman, and Fiona Murray, MIx helps startups understand defense customers' needs and navigate government engagement challenges. Through MIx, innovators receive funding and defense agencies gain access to cutting-edge technologies with dual civilian and defense applications. The program, rooted in MIT's history of collaboration with the Department of Defense, aims to enhance America's security capabilities while fostering innovation. MIx's early successes include projects with startups like Picogrid and Nominal, demonstrating the program's potential to drive future collaborations and advancements in defense technology.
