Nominal is an advanced software platform designed for engineers working on complex systems like fighter jets, nuclear reactors, and robotics. It helps organize, visualize, and share test data, as well as build custom applications for hardware systems. The platform aims to accelerate hardware development by providing a unified database for real-time data organization, enabling engineers to analyze trends, identify events, and investigate anomalies. Nominal's cloud-hosted platform automates tasks, accelerates post-test analysis, and facilitates collaboration within engineering teams. The company recently secured a $75 million funding round to expand its product offerings and team. By implementing a data-driven design process, Nominal aims to revolutionize hardware development with continuous testing and feedback loops, similar to practices in the software industry.
