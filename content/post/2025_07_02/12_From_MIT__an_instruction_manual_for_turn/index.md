title = 'From MIT, an instruction manual for turning research into startups'
+++

MIT Proto Ventures launched the "R&D Venture Studio Playbook" to help universities and R&D offices establish their own venture studios. The playbook offers a framework for building ventures from research environments systematically. It aims to address the challenge of commercializing academic patents and make venture-building a systemic process. The playbook outlines MIT's venture studio model, which embeds entrepreneurial scientists in research labs to scout technologies, validate market opportunities, and co-create ventures. It emphasizes the need for deliberate focus and a new translational infrastructure to build successful ventures from R&D. The playbook includes case studies, templates, and guidance for institutions to customize the model. The goal is to inspire innovation and accelerate the transition of ideas from the lab to real-world solutions.
