title = 'Four from MIT named 2025 Goldwater Scholars'
+++

MIT has four rising seniors who received the 2025 Barry Goldwater Scholarship: Avani Ahuja and Jacqueline Prawira from the School of Engineering, and Julianna Lian and Alex Tang from the School of Science. The scholarships support undergraduates aiming to become leading scientists, engineers, and mathematicians. Each student is conducting impactful research in various fields, such as developing wearable ultrasound patches, studying mechanochemistry, researching sustainable materials, and investigating immunotherapy effects on cancer patients. They all have ambitious career goals and are grateful for the mentorship and support they have received. The Goldwater Scholarship aims to encourage research careers in science, engineering, and mathematics and is a prestigious award in these fields.
